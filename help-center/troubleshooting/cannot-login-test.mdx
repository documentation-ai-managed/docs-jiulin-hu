---
title: Cannot Log In to Jiulin Hu Test
description: Resolve login problems quickly.
---

## Symptoms

- Login page reloads
- Error: "Invalid credentials"

## Causes

- Wrong email or password
- Browser cache issue

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset Password">
    Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and check your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache">
    Clear cookies and cache, then retry logging in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
